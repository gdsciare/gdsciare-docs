# UI/UX Design Resources

![](public/images/resources/uiux_banner.png)

Welcome to the UI/UX Design Resources section curated for beginner students by our UI UX Team! Here, you'll find a collection of fantastic resources that can help you kickstart your journey in UI/UX design, find inspiration, and learn the principles of effective design.

1. [Dribbble](https://dribbble.com/) - A platform for designers to showcase their work, explore design trends, and find inspiration from a vast collection of UI/UX designs.

2. [Behance](https://www.behance.net/) - An online platform to discover and showcase creative work, including UI/UX design projects. Behance allows you to explore a wide range of design styles and get inspired by talented designers worldwide.

3. [UX Design Weekly](https://uxdesignweekly.com/) - A curated newsletter that delivers a collection of handpicked articles, resources, and design inspirations related to UI/UX design straight to your inbox.

4. [Nielsen Norman Group](https://www.nngroup.com/) - A leading UX research and consulting firm. Their website offers a wealth of articles, research reports, and usability guidelines that cover various aspects of UX design.

5. [Designmodo](https://designmodo.com/) - An online resource that provides design tutorials, UI kits, and articles on UI/UX design best practices. It's a great place to learn fundamental design principles and stay updated with the latest design trends.

6. [UX Booth](https://www.uxbooth.com/) - An online publication that publishes articles on user experience design, usability, and interaction design. It covers a wide range of topics, making it a valuable resource for beginner UX designers.

7. [Awwwards](https://www.awwwards.com/) - A platform that showcases award-winning websites, providing inspiration and insights into cutting-edge UI/UX design. Explore their gallery and learn from the best in the industry.

8. [InVision Blog](https://www.invisionapp.com/blog/) - The blog of InVision, a popular design collaboration platform. The blog features articles, tutorials, and interviews with designers, covering various aspects of UI/UX design.

9. [CSS Gradients](https://cssgradient.io/) - CSS Gradients is a website that offers a collection of gradient examples and a gradient generator tool. It's a valuable resource for creating beautiful color gradients for your UI designs.

10. [Canva Design School](https://www.canva.com/learn/) - Canva's Design School provides tutorials, tips, and resources for graphic design and UI/UX design. It's a beginner-friendly resource with practical guidance for creating visually appealing designs.
